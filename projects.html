User Research for YouTube Music

A usability research project to identify user expectations and pain points, when interacting with YouTube Music across connected devices and navigating the in-app voice experience.


Artifacts include various research reports, synthesizing team findings and recommendations from data collected through surveys, user interviews, comparative evaluation, and usability tests.

Outreach for Winning Futures

A contextual inquiry project to improve the client's digital communications strategy and website design, in efforts to increase stakeholder engagement.


Artifacts include a final research report, based on findings gathered through contextual inquiry and market analysis.

Onboarding for Remote Hires

A mobile application design project to streamline the process of completing and submitting Form I-9, specifically for remote hires.


Artifacts were developed throughout the design process and include sketches, storyboards, user personas, storymaps, design rationale analyses, and paper and digital prototypes of the final interface.

Wharton Website Evaluation

A series of quantitative analyses conducted to identify opportunities for design improvement, site optimization, and tailored marketing initiatives.


Artifacts include presentations synthesizing data collected via Google Analytics dashboards, user interviews, and A/B tests.

Rehabilitation Through the Arts

A J.P. Morgan Chase Force For Good project to develop an internal communications platform for non-profit organization, Rehabilitation Through the Arts, to help better manage administrative processes.

Please note that artifacts cannot be shared, due to Intellectual Property rights of J.P. Morgan Chase.
